Our classes

DoBu Martial Arts offers a wide range of classes in different martial arts, as well as fitness training and self-defence courses. We have classes for all ages and skill levels, so whether you're a beginner or an experienced martial artist, we have something for you.

Judo

A grappling martial art focusing on throws, holds and submissions.

Karate

A striking martial art using punches, kicks and defensive techniques.

Jiu-Jitsu

A ground-based martial art focusing on submissions and control.

Muay Thai

A powerful striking art using fists, elbows, knees and kicks.

Kids Classes

Fun and structured martial arts sessions designed for children.

Fitness Training

Strength and conditioning programs to improve overall fitness.
