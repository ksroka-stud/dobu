About DoBu Martial Arts

DoBu Martial Arts is a modern training facility dedicated to helping members improve fitness, confidence and martial arts skills. We welcome beginners, experienced athletes and families looking for professional coaching in a safe and supportive environment.

Our gym includes a large matted martial arts area, fully equipped fitness gym, sauna, steam room and changing facilities.

All skill levels

From complete beginners to advanced martial artists, everyone is welcome.
